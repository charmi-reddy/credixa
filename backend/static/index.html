Three Role Flow

Credixa

Choose whether you are the Supplier, Investor, or Buyer. Each role has its own login page and separate workspace.

Supplier

Needs Money

Create invoices for buyers and request early funding.
Investor

Funds Discounted Invoices

Provide a lower upfront amount and hold the invoice until buyer repayment.
Buyer

Pays Later

Track invoices assigned to you and see what is owed after funding.
